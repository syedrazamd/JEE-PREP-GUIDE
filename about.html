Our Journey

How JEE Prep Guide came to life

2022

The Idea

During our mentoring sessions with underprivileged JEE aspirants, we realized that lack of quality study material was the biggest barrier. Most free resources online were incomplete, outdated, or filled with errors. That's when we decided to create something different.

2023

The Launch

After months of research and content creation, we launched JEE Prep Guide with 10 comprehensive Physics chapters. The response was overwhelming - within the first month, over 1,000 students started using our notes.

2024

Rapid Growth

We expanded to complete Physics coverage and saw 100+ students who used our notes crack JEE Main with 95+ percentile. Their success stories became our motivation to work even harder.

2025

The Future

We're launching complete Chemistry and Mathematics notes, adding interactive practice questions, video explanations, and building a community of 50,000+ JEE aspirants. The journey has just begun!
