Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Is this really completely free?

Yes! All our study material is 100% free forever. No hidden costs, no subscriptions, no premium plans. We believe quality education should be accessible to everyone.

❓ How can I report an error in the notes?

Use the contact form above and select "Content Error/Issue" as the subject. Please mention the chapter name and specific error location. We review and fix errors within 48 hours.

❓ Can I download notes as PDF?

We're working on adding PDF download functionality. For now, you can use your browser's "Print to PDF" feature (Ctrl+P) to save any page offline.

❓ When will Chemistry and Maths notes be available?

Chemistry notes are coming in January 2025 and Mathematics in February 2025. Join our Telegram channel to get notified when new content is released.

❓ How can I contribute or suggest improvements?

We love feedback! Use the contact form with subject "Feedback & Suggestions". We review all suggestions and implement the most requested features.

❓ Do you offer one-on-one doubt solving?

Currently, we don't offer individual tutoring. However, you can post your doubts in our Telegram community where fellow students and moderators help each other.

Still have questions?

Don't hesitate to reach out. We're here to help you succeed!
