RBI Office Attendant Exam Pattern 2026

Understanding the exam pattern is crucial for effective preparation. Here's the complete structure of the RBI Office Attendant examination:

Numerical Ability 40 40 45 Minutes
Reasoning Ability 40 40
General Awareness 40 40
TOTAL 120 120 45 Minutes

📌 Important Exam Rules

  • ✓ Each question carries 1 mark
  • ✓ Negative marking: 1/4 mark deducted for each wrong answer
  • ✓ No penalty for unattempted questions
  • ✓ All questions are multiple choice (MCQs)
  • ✓ Each question has 5 options (a, b, c, d, e)
  • ✓ Online computer-based test (CBT)

🎯 Qualifying Criteria

  • ✓ Candidates must score minimum qualifying marks
  • ✓ Sectional cut-off applies (each section separately)
  • ✓ Overall cut-off must also be cleared
  • ✓ Cut-off varies by category (SC/ST/OBC/Gen)
  • ✓ Final merit list based on total score
  • ✓ Selected candidates called for language test/document verification

📚 Section-wise Syllabus Overview

1️⃣ Numerical Ability:

Simplification, Number Series, Data Interpretation, Percentage, Profit & Loss, Simple & Compound Interest, Time & Work, Time & Distance, Average, Ratio & Proportion, Mensuration, etc.

2️⃣ Reasoning Ability:

Analogies, Similarities, Differences, Coding-Decoding, Blood Relations, Directions, Syllogism, Puzzles, Seating Arrangement, Order & Ranking, Alpha-Numeric Series, etc.

3️⃣ General Awareness:

Current Affairs (last 6 months), Banking Awareness, RBI Functions, Indian Economy, Awards & Honours, Sports, Books & Authors, Important Days, National & International Events, etc.

Exam Day Instructions & Guidelines

Following these guidelines will ensure smooth entry and comfortable exam experience:

🌅 Before the Exam Day

  • ✓ Visit exam center location at least one day before to avoid last-minute rush
  • ✓ Check route & distance: Plan your travel time considering traffic
  • ✓ Keep documents ready: Admit card, ID proof, photographs in a transparent folder
  • ✓ Sleep well: Get at least 7-8 hours of sleep before exam
  • ✓ Prepare clothes: Comfortable clothing without metal accessories

📅 On the Exam Day

  • ✓ Arrive early: Reach venue at least 60-90 minutes before reporting time
  • ✓ Eat light breakfast: Avoid heavy meals that may cause discomfort
  • ✓ Carry water bottle: Stay hydrated (transparent bottle only)
  • ✓ Wear simple clothes: No belts with metal buckles, minimal jewelry
  • ✓ Check admit card again: Verify all details one last time

🏢 At the Exam Center

  • ✓ Security check mandatory: Submit to frisking and bag checking
  • ✓ Biometric verification: Fingerprint & photograph will be captured
  • ✓ Document verification: Original ID proof will be checked against admit card
  • ✓ Follow instructions: Listen carefully to invigilator's announcements
  • ✓ Check your seat: Find your designated computer terminal
  • ✓ Test computer: Verify mouse, keyboard functioning before exam starts

⏱️ During the Examination

  • ✓ Read instructions carefully: Understand exam pattern & marking scheme
  • ✓ Manage time wisely: Don't spend too much time on one question
  • ✓ Attempt easy questions first: Build confidence and secure marks
  • ✓ Be careful with negative marking: Avoid wild guessing
  • ✓ Use review feature: Mark questions for review if unsure
  • ✓ Keep calm: Don't panic if questions seem difficult
  • ✓ Submit properly: Click submit button before time ends

⛔ Strict Rules - Violation May Lead to Disqualification

  • ❌ No malpractice or unfair means allowed
  • ❌ No communication with other candidates during exam
  • ❌ No mobile phones or electronic devices in exam hall
  • ❌ Cannot leave exam hall before completion
  • ❌ Rough work only on provided sheets (not on admit card)
  • ❌ Any suspicious behavior will be reported
  • ❌ CCTV surveillance throughout the exam

RBI Office Attendant Selection Process 2026

The complete selection process for RBI Office Attendant 2026 consists of multiple stages:

1

Preliminary Examination (Online)

Computer-based test consisting of Numerical Ability, Reasoning, and General Awareness

  • • Duration: 45 minutes
  • • Total Questions: 120
  • • Negative marking applicable
  • • This is the current stage - Admit Card Released
2

Language Proficiency Test (LPT)

Qualifying test in local/official language of the state

  • • Only for shortlisted candidates from prelims
  • • Tests reading & writing proficiency
  • • Must qualify to proceed further
  • • Date will be announced later
3

Document Verification & Medical Exam

Final verification of documents and medical fitness

  • • Original documents verification
  • • Educational certificates, caste certificate, etc.
  • • Medical fitness examination
  • • Final selection after successful verification

🎯 Final Merit List

The final merit list will be prepared based on the performance in Preliminary Examination only. Language Proficiency Test is qualifying in nature. Selected candidates will be appointed as Office Attendant in various RBI offices across India based on vacancy and preference.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

❓ How to download RBI Office Attendant Admit Card 2026? ⌄

Visit the official website opportunities.rbi.org.in, click on "Current Vacancies", select "Office Attendant" recruitment link, login with your Registration Number and Password, and download your admit card. Take a printout for the exam day.

❓ What is the RBI Office Attendant exam date 2026? ⌄

The RBI Office Attendant preliminary examination is scheduled to be held in February 2026. The exact date and time slot is mentioned on your individual admit card. Please check your admit card for specific details.

❓ What documents are required for RBI Office Attendant exam? ⌄

You must carry: (1) Printed admit card, (2) Valid photo ID proof (Aadhaar/PAN/Driving License/Passport/Voter ID) - original and photocopy, (3) One recent passport size photograph. Without these, entry will be denied.

❓ Is there negative marking in RBI Office Attendant exam? ⌄

Yes, there is negative marking. For each wrong answer, 1/4 mark (0.25 marks) will be deducted. There is no penalty for unattempted questions. So attempt only those questions you are confident about.

❓ Can I download the admit card from mobile? ⌄

Yes, you can download from mobile, but it's recommended to use a laptop or desktop for better experience. Use Chrome or Firefox browser. Make sure to save the PDF file and take a proper printout from a printer.

❓ What is the exam duration for RBI Office Attendant? ⌄

The total duration of the preliminary examination is 45 minutes. There are 120 questions covering Numerical Ability, Reasoning Ability, and General Awareness sections. All questions are of multiple choice type.

❓ What if I forgot my registration number? ⌄

Check your registered email or SMS for the registration number. You can also find it in your application confirmation page. If still not found, use the "Forgot Registration Number" link on the admit card download page (you'll need your registered email/mobile).

❓ Is the exam conducted online or offline? ⌄

The RBI Office Attendant examination is conducted online in computer-based test (CBT) mode. You will answer questions on a computer screen. Basic computer knowledge of using mouse and keyboard is sufficient.

❓ Can I carry my own stationery to the exam center? ⌄

No, you cannot carry any stationery items. Rough work sheets (if required) will be provided by the exam center. You don't need pen/pencil as it's a computer-based test. Carrying any stationery may lead to disqualification.

❓ When will RBI Office Attendant result be declared? ⌄

The RBI Office Attendant preliminary exam result will be declared approximately 2-3 weeks after the examination. The exact date will be announced on the official website. Results will be available online at opportunities.rbi.org.in.
