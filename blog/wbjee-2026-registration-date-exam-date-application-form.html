1. What is WBJEE?

WBJEE full form is West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination. It is a state-level entrance exam conducted by WBJEEB (West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board) for admission to undergraduate courses in Engineering, Technology, Pharmacy, and Architecture in colleges across West Bengal.

💡 Key Facts About WBJEE

  • 🎓 Exam Level: State Level (West Bengal)
  • 🏫 Courses: Engineering, Pharmacy, Architecture
  • 📝 Exam Mode: Offline (OMR-based pen & paper)
  • 📚 Subjects: Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry
  • ⚠️ Negative Marking: Yes (varies by category)
  • 🗓️ Exam Frequency: Once a year

4. How to Register for WBJEE 2026 (Step-by-Step)

⚠️ Registration Window: March 10 – April 5, 2026. Don't wait until the last day!

Step 1: Visit Official Website

Go to wbjeeb.nic.in and click on "WBJEE 2026 Registration" link.

Step 2: Fill Registration Form

Enter the following details:

  • Full Name (as per Class 10 certificate)
  • Date of Birth
  • Gender, Category (General/SC/ST/OBC)
  • Mobile Number & Email ID (verify with OTP)
  • Parent/Guardian Details
  • Address & State of Eligibility

Step 3: Upload Documents

Upload scanned copies (in JPG/JPEG format):

  • Photograph: Passport size, recent, 10–200 KB
  • Signature: On white paper with black/blue pen, 4–30 KB
  • Thumb Impression: Left thumb (for males), Right thumb (for females)

Step 4: Select Exam Centers

Choose your preferred exam city. WBJEE centers are available across West Bengal including Kolkata, Durgapur, Siliguri, Asansol, Burdwan, etc.

Step 5: Pay Application Fee

Payment modes: Net Banking, Credit Card, Debit Card, UPI

💡 Keep screenshot of payment confirmation!

Step 6: Submit & Download

After successful payment, download and print your Application Form and Fee Receipt. Keep them safe until admission process completes.

📌 Important Notes:

  • ✅ One candidate can submit only ONE application. Duplicate applications will be rejected.
  • ✅ Form correction window: April 7-9, 2026. You can edit Name, DOB, Category, etc.
  • ✅ No offline applications accepted.
  • ✅ Keep your login credentials (Application No. & Password) safe for future use.

6. WBJEE 2026 Eligibility Criteria

🎓 For Engineering/Technology

  • ✓ Pass 10+2 or equivalent with Physics, Mathematics as compulsory subjects
  • ✓ One of: Chemistry / Biology / Biotechnology / Computer Science
  • ✓ English pass with minimum 30% marks
  • ✓ Minimum aggregate: 45% (Gen), 40% (SC/ST/OBC/PwD)

💊 For Pharmacy

  • ✓ Pass 10+2 with Physics and Chemistry as compulsory
  • ✓ Plus: Mathematics OR Biology
  • ✓ English pass with minimum 30% marks
  • ✓ Minimum aggregate: 45% (Gen), 40% (SC/ST/OBC/PwD)

📅 Age Criteria

Minimum Age: 17 years as of December 31, 2026

This means you must be born on or before December 31, 2009

Maximum Age: No upper age limit (except Marine Engineering: 25 years)

⚠️ Important Points:

  • ❌ Students appearing in 10+2 final exams in 2026 are also eligible
  • ❌ Candidates with compartment/improvement are NOT eligible
  • ❌ Diploma holders: Not eligible for WBJEE (but can apply through lateral entry)
  • ✅ Foreign nationals/NRI/PIO are eligible but will compete in General category

💡 Pro Strategy Tips:

  • ✅ Mathematics weightage is 50% — Focus maximum time here
  • ✅ For Category 3 (multiple correct), attempt only if you're 100% sure to avoid zero marks
  • ✅ Category 2 has higher penalty (-0.50) — Don't guess randomly
  • ✅ Time management: Spend ~70 minutes on Maths, ~50 minutes on Physics & Chemistry each
  • ✅ Since exam is offline (OMR), practice filling bubbles neatly and quickly

  • ✅ GMR (General Merit Rank)
  • ✅ PMR (Pharmacy Merit Rank) (if applicable)
  • ✅ Category Rank (SC/ST/OBC, if applicable)
  • ✅ Percentile Score

🏆 How WBJEE Rank is Calculated:

1. Mathematics weightage is higher:

  • • If two candidates have same total, the one with higher Maths score gets better rank

2. If Maths score is also same:

  • • Physics score is compared

3. If still tied:

  • • Chemistry score is compared

4. Final tiebreaker:

  • • Older candidate (by DOB) gets better rank

  • ✅ Download & save multiple copies of Rank Card

14. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

❓ What is WBJEE 2026 registration date?

WBJEE 2026 registration starts on March 10, 2026 and closes on April 5, 2026. Apply online at wbjeeb.nic.in.

❓ What is WBJEE 2026 exam date?

WBJEE 2026 exam date is May 24, 2026 (Sunday). Paper I (Mathematics) from 11 AM-1 PM, Paper II (Physics & Chemistry) from 2 PM-4 PM.

❓ What is WBJEE 2026 registration last date?

WBJEE 2026 registration last date is April 5, 2026. Form correction window will be open from April 7-9, 2026.

❓ What is WBJEE 2026 registration fees?

WBJEE 2026 registration fees: ₹500 for General Male, ₹400 for General Female/SC/ST/OBC Male, ₹300 for SC/ST/OBC Female, ₹200-300 for Third Gender. Payment via Net Banking/Credit/Debit Card.

❓ What is WBJEE eligibility criteria?

WBJEE eligibility: Must be 17+ years (born on/before Dec 31, 2009). For Engineering: 10+2 with Physics, Mathematics + Chemistry/Biology/CS/Biotech. Minimum 45% aggregate (40% for SC/ST/OBC/PwD). English pass with 30%+ marks.

❓ Is there negative marking in WBJEE?

Yes. Category 1: -0.25 for wrong. Category 2: -0.50 for wrong. Category 3 (multiple correct): No negative marking.

❓ Can I edit my application after submission?

Yes, during the correction window (April 7-9, 2026). You can edit Name, DOB, Category, Photo, etc. No changes allowed after this period.

❓ Can students appearing in Class 12 apply for WBJEE?

Yes! Students appearing in Class 12 final exams in 2026 are eligible. But you must pass with required percentage before admission.

❓ Is WBJEE exam online or offline?

WBJEE is an offline exam (OMR-based pen & paper test). You'll mark answers on an OMR sheet.

❓ Which colleges accept WBJEE score?

100+ Government & Private Engineering/Pharmacy colleges in West Bengal including Jadavpur University, IIEST Shibpur, Kalyani Govt. Engineering College, Heritage Institute, Techno India, etc.

❓ When will WBJEE 2026 admit card be released?

WBJEE 2026 admit card will be available from May 15, 2026 onwards on wbjeeb.nic.in.

❓ When will WBJEE 2026 result be declared?

WBJEE 2026 result will be declared in the 3rd or 4th week of June 2026.

❓ How many choices can I fill in WBJEE counselling?

You can fill 100+ choices (colleges & branches) during WBJEE counselling. Fill maximum to increase chances.

❓ What is the difference between GMR and PMR in WBJEE?

GMR (General Merit Rank) is for Engineering admissions. PMR (Pharmacy Merit Rank) is for B.Pharm admissions. Both are calculated based on your scores.
