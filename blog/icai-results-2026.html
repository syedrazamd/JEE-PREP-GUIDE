3. How to Check CA Final Result January 2026 at icai.nic.in — Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to check your ICAI CA Final result or CA Inter result January 2026 from the official website:

1

Visit the Official ICAI Website

Open your browser and go to icai.nic.in or icaiexam.icai.org or caresults.icai.org

2

Click on the Result Link

On the homepage, look for "CA Final Result January 2026" or "CA Inter Result January 2026" link and click on it.

3

Enter Your Credentials

Enter your Registration Number or PIN Number in the required fields. You may also need to enter your Roll Number.

4

Submit and View Result

Click the "Submit" button. Your CA Final result or CA Inter result will appear on the screen with subject-wise marks and pass/fail status.

5

Download & Save Scorecard

Download your scorecard as PDF and take a printout for future reference. The official mark sheet will be sent by post from ICAI.

5. CA Final Passing Criteria — January 2026

To pass the CA Final examination, candidates must fulfil the following criteria set by ICAI:

Criteria Requirement
Minimum per Paper 40 marks out of 100 (40%) in each individual paper
Minimum Aggregate (Per Group) 50% aggregate in all papers of the respective group
Both Groups (If appearing together) Must pass both groups — 40% per paper + 50% aggregate in each group
Exemption Scoring 60% or above in a paper grants exemption for next attempt
Maximum Marks 100 marks per paper (Total: 400 per group, 800 both groups)

9. What After CA Final Result? — Next Steps

✅ If You PASSED CA Final

  • 🎉 Congratulations! You are now a Chartered Accountant
  • 📝 Apply for ICAI membership within the specified period
  • 📜 Complete GMCS (General Management & Communication Skills) training if pending
  • 🏢 Start applying at Big 4 firms (Deloitte, PwC, EY, KPMG) or other CA firms
  • 📚 Consider pursuing CFA, CPA, or MBA for additional qualifications
  • 💼 Explore opportunities in corporate finance, taxation, auditing, consulting

❌ If You Did NOT Pass

  • 💪 Don't lose hope! Many toppers cleared in 2nd or 3rd attempt
  • 📊 Analyze your scorecard — Identify weak areas
  • 📝 Check if you got exemption (60%+) in any paper
  • 📅 Register for the next attempt (May 2026) immediately
  • 📖 Change your study strategy — Focus on weak subjects
  • 🔄 Apply for verification/re-evaluation if marks seem incorrect (within 30 days)
