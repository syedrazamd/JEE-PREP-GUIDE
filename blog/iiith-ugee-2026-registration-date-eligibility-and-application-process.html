1. What is UGEE? Full Form, Meaning & Overview

UGEE full form is Undergraduate Entrance Examination. It is the official entrance exam conducted by the International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad (IIIT Hyderabad or IIITH) for admission to its 4-year B.Tech and 5-year Dual Degree (B.Tech + M.S. by Research) programs.

IIIT Hyderabad is one of India's most prestigious institutes for Computer Science, AI, Machine Learning, and IT. It is consistently ranked among the top engineering colleges in India. Unlike IITs (JEE Advanced) or NITs (JEE Main), IIIT Hyderabad conducts its own entrance exam — UGEE — and offers three different admission channels:

🔀 Three Admission Channels in UGEE

  • 🔬 SUPR (Specialize in Undergraduate Research) — Based on UGEE written test + interview
  • 📊 REAP (Research and Engineering Aptitude Program) — Based on JEE Main score + interview
  • 🏆 Lateral Entry — For students with Olympiad medals, KVPY fellowship, published research papers, or national-level competition achievements — interview only

This means even if you don't take the UGEE written test, you can still get into IIIT Hyderabad through your JEE Main score (REAP) or through exceptional achievements (Lateral Entry). All three channels lead to the same B.Tech and Dual Degree programs.

💡 Why IIIT Hyderabad is Special

  • 🔬 Research-oriented from Day 1 — Unique research-based curriculum
  • 💰 Outstanding placements — Among the best in India for CS/IT
  • 🔀 Multiple admission paths — SUPR, REAP, and Lateral Entry
  • 👥 Small batches — Personalized attention and mentoring
  • 🌐 Global alumni network — Graduates at Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Meta, and top startups
  • 🤖 Strong in AI/ML — One of India's best institutes for AI and Data Science research

4. UGEE 2026 Registration — How to Apply (Step-by-Step)

The UGEE 2026 registration process is completely online through the official portal. Here's the step-by-step guide:

1

Visit the Official UGEE Portal

Go to ugee.iiit.ac.in and click on "Apply Now" or "New Registration".

2

Create Account & Verify

Register with valid email and mobile number. Verify via OTP. Create a strong password and save your credentials.

3

Select Admission Channel(s)

Choose from SUPR, REAP, and/or Lateral Entry. You can select multiple channels to maximize your chances.

4

Fill Personal & Academic Details

Enter your name (as per Class 10 marksheet), DOB, parent details, Class 12 board, marks/percentage, and other academic information.

5

Upload Documents

Upload passport-size photograph, signature, and any supporting documents (Olympiad certificates for Lateral Entry, etc.).

6

Select Exam Centre (for SUPR)

If applying through SUPR channel, select your preferred exam centre city. Choose at least 2-3 preferences.

7

Pay Application Fee

Pay ₹1,000 (Male Gen/OBC/EWS) or ₹500 (Female/SC/ST/PwD) via net banking, credit/debit card, or UPI.

8

Review & Submit

Carefully review all information. Once submitted, most fields cannot be changed. Download and save the confirmation page.

⚠️ Registration Tips

  • • Apply for multiple channels (SUPR + REAP + Lateral if eligible) to maximize chances
  • • Name must match your Class 10 certificate exactly
  • • Don't wait for the last date — IIIT Hyderabad rarely extends deadlines
  • • If applying for REAP, keep your JEE Main registration/roll number ready
  • • For Lateral Entry, upload supporting achievement certificates

The UGEE eligibility criteria vary slightly based on the admission channel. Here's the complete breakdown:

General Eligibility (All Channels)

Criteria Requirement
Educational Qualification Passed or appearing in Class 12 (or equivalent) in 2025 or 2026
Mandatory Subject Mathematics is compulsory in Class 12
Minimum Percentage No minimum percentage required to appear
Accepted Boards CBSE, ICSE/ISC, All State Boards, IB, Cambridge, NIOS, or any recognized board
Nationality Indian Nationals (International students have separate process)

Channel-wise Additional Requirements

Channel Additional Requirement
SUPR Must appear for UGEE written test. No JEE Main score required.
REAP Must have a valid JEE Main 2026 score. No UGEE written test required.
Lateral Entry Must have exceptional achievements: Olympiad medals (IMO, IPhO, IChO, IOI), KVPY fellowship, published research papers, or wins in national-level science/tech competitions. Supporting documents required.

✅ Key Point: Mathematics is the only mandatory subject — unlike many entrance exams that require PCM. However, having Physics and Chemistry background is recommended. There is no minimum percentage requirement to appear for UGEE.

7. Admission Channels — SUPR vs REAP vs Lateral Entry

This is the most important section. IIIT Hyderabad has three distinct admission channels, each with different requirements:

🔬 SUPR

Specialize in Undergraduate Research

  • 📝 Test: UGEE Written Test required
  • 🎤 Interview: Yes
  • ❌ JEE Main: Not required
  • 📊 Selection: UGEE Score + Interview
  • 🎯 Best For: Strong in math & reasoning

📊 REAP

Research & Engineering Aptitude Program

  • 📝 Test: No UGEE test needed
  • 🎤 Interview: Yes
  • ✅ JEE Main: Required (valid score)
  • 📊 Selection: JEE Main Score + Interview
  • 🎯 Best For: Strong JEE Main performers

🏆 Lateral Entry

For Exceptional Achievers

  • 📝 Test: No test required
  • 🎤 Interview: Yes
  • ❌ JEE Main: Not required
  • 📊 Selection: Achievement + Interview
  • 🎯 Best For: Olympiad / KVPY / Research achievers

Who Qualifies for Lateral Entry?

Lateral Entry is for students with exceptional achievements in any of the following:

  • 🥇International Olympiad medals — IMO, IPhO, IChO, IOI, or equivalent
  • 🔬KVPY Fellowship — Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana
  • 📄Published Research Papers — In recognized journals or conferences
  • 🏆National-level competition wins — Science fairs, tech competitions, hackathons, etc.
  • 💻Significant technical projects/contributions — Open source, apps, etc.

💡 Strategy: You can apply through all three channels simultaneously. If you have a JEE Main score AND want to take the UGEE test AND have Olympiad achievements — apply for all three. This gives you the maximum chance of getting admitted.

8. UGEE 2026 Exam Pattern & Marking Scheme

The UGEE written test (for SUPR channel only) has the following pattern:

Parameter Details
Exam Mode Computer Based Test (CBT) — Online at exam centres
Duration 3 Hours (180 minutes)
Question Types Multiple Choice Questions (MCQ) + Numerical Answer Type (NAT)
Subjects Mathematics, Analytical & Logical Reasoning, English Comprehension
Negative Marking (MCQ) Yes — 1/3rd of marks deducted for each wrong MCQ answer
Negative Marking (NAT) No negative marking for NAT questions
Language English only

Subject-wise Weightage (Approximate)

Section Topics Weightage
Mathematics Algebra, Calculus, Coordinate Geometry, Trigonometry, Probability, Combinatorics 50-60%
Analytical & Logical Reasoning Puzzles, Pattern Recognition, Data Interpretation, Logical Deduction 25-30%
English Comprehension Reading Comprehension, Grammar, Vocabulary 10-15%

⚠️ Key Difference from JEE: UGEE does NOT test Physics or Chemistry. The exam focuses entirely on Mathematics, Reasoning, and English. This requires a different preparation approach. For detailed prep strategy, check our UGEE Exam Pattern & Syllabus Guide.

10. UGEE 2026 Selection Process

The selection process varies by channel but all channels include a mandatory personal interview:

SUPR Channel Selection

  1. Step 1: Appear for UGEE Written Test (CBT)
  2. Step 2: Shortlisting based on UGEE written test score
  3. Step 3: Personal Interview (for shortlisted candidates)
  4. Step 4: Final Merit List (Written Test Score + Interview Score)
  5. Step 5: Seat Allotment & Admission

REAP Channel Selection

  1. Step 1: Submit valid JEE Main 2026 score
  2. Step 2: Shortlisting based on JEE Main score/rank
  3. Step 3: Personal Interview (for shortlisted candidates)
  4. Step 4: Final Merit List (JEE Main Score + Interview Score)
  5. Step 5: Seat Allotment & Admission

Lateral Entry Selection

  1. Step 1: Submit application with achievement certificates
  2. Step 2: Screening of achievements by IIITH committee
  3. Step 3: Personal Interview (for shortlisted candidates)
  4. Step 4: Final selection based on interview performance
  5. Step 5: Seat Allotment & Admission

About the Interview

  • ⏱️ Duration: 15-30 minutes
  • 👨‍🏫 Panel: 2-3 IIIT Hyderabad professors
  • 📚 Topics: Math puzzles, logical reasoning, motivation, research interest, tech awareness
  • 💡 Tip: They test your thought process, not just final answers. Think out loud!
  • 🗣️ Language: Primarily English
