1. What is COMEDK UGET? Full Form & Overview

COMEDK UGET full form is Consortium of Medical, Engineering and Dental Colleges of Karnataka - Undergraduate Entrance Test. It is the state-level entrance examination conducted by COMEDK for admission to B.E. / B.Tech programs in over 180+ private engineering colleges in Karnataka.

COMEDK UGET is one of the most popular engineering entrance exams in South India. Every year, lakhs of students appear for this exam to secure admission in top private engineering colleges in Karnataka like RV College of Engineering (RVCE), BMS College of Engineering, MS Ramaiah Institute of Technology, PES University, Dayananda Sagar College, and many more.

💡 Key Facts About COMEDK UGET

  • 🎓 Exam Level: State Level (Karnataka) — but open to students from ALL states
  • 🏫 Participating Colleges: 180+ private engineering colleges in Karnataka
  • 📝 Exam Mode: Computer Based Test (CBT) — Online
  • 📚 Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics (PCM)
  • ✅ No Negative Marking: Unlike JEE Main, COMEDK has NO negative marking!
  • 🌍 Open to All States: Students from any Indian state can apply
  • 📅 Exam Frequency: Once a year (usually in May)

Note: COMEDK is separate from KCET (Karnataka Common Entrance Test). KCET is for government engineering colleges in Karnataka, while COMEDK is for private engineering colleges. Many students appear for both exams to maximize their options.

4. COMEDK 2026 Registration — How to Apply (Step-by-Step)

Follow these steps to complete your COMEDK 2026 registration:

1

Visit the Registration Portal

Go to the official COMEDK Applicant Registration portal at comedk.org and click on "Register" or "Apply Online".

2

Generate User ID & Password

Enter your basic details (name, email, mobile number). You'll receive User ID and Password via OTP on your registered mobile and email. Save these credentials.

3

Login & Fill Application Form

Login with your credentials and complete the application form with personal details, academic details, and category details.

4

Upload Documents

Upload scanned copies of: Photograph, Signature, Parent's Signature, and ID Proof. File size: Max 80-160 KB in JPEG format.

5

Select Preferred Exam Cities

Choose THREE preferred exam centre cities. Centre allotment is based on availability and your preference order.

6

Pay Registration Fee

Pay the fee online via Credit Card, Debit Card, or Net Banking. COMEDK UGET Only: ₹1,950. UGET + Uni-GAUGE-E: ₹3,200 (+ convenience charges).

7

Submit & Download Confirmation

Review all details carefully. Once satisfied, submit the application and download the confirmation page for future reference.

⚠️ Important Registration Tips

  • • Use a valid email and mobile — All communication will be sent here
  • • Upload clear documents — Blurry images may lead to rejection
  • • Don't wait until the last date — Server may be slow due to traffic
  • • Save credentials safely — You'll need them for admit card and result
  • • Take a printout of the confirmation page

Uni-GAUGE-E is a national-level engineering entrance exam conducted by COMEDK. It is accepted by several private universities and engineering colleges outside Karnataka for B.Tech admissions.

7. COMEDK 2026 Eligibility Criteria

Before applying, ensure you meet all the COMEDK eligibility criteria:

Criteria Requirement
Educational Qualification Passed or appearing in 10+2 / Second PUC / Equivalent examination
Compulsory Subjects Physics, Mathematics, and English are mandatory
Third Subject (Any One) Chemistry, Bio-Technology, Biology, Computer Science, or Electronics

Diploma Holders ❌ NOT ELIGIBLE — No lateral entry admissions

⚠️ Important: Diploma Holders Not Eligible

Diploma holders are NOT eligible for COMEDK UGET as there are no lateral entry admissions through this exam. COMEDK is only for candidates who have passed or are appearing in Class 12 / PUC / equivalent.

✅ Advantage: No Negative Marking!

Unlike JEE Main (which has -1 for wrong answers), COMEDK has NO negative marking. This means you should attempt all 180 questions even if you're unsure. There's no penalty for guessing!

14. COMEDK 2026 Counselling Process

After the result is declared, qualified candidates will participate in COMEDK counselling for seat allotment:

  1. Step 1: Registration for Counselling — Register on the counselling portal with your COMEDK credentials
  2. Step 2: Document Verification — Upload required documents for verification

  6. Step 6: Report to College — Visit the allotted college with original documents

⚠️ Keep all original documents ready for counselling: Class 10 & 12 marksheets, Transfer Certificate, Migration Certificate, Category Certificate (if applicable), etc.
